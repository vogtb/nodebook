Nodebook is a visually intuitive way to organize of your ideas.

Nodes are piece of text, and the connection between them represents words they have in common. For example if one node has the text:

There are too many fish in my tank.
and another node has the text:
The ocean has a wide variety of fish.
They would be a connection between the two, because they contain the word "fish."
